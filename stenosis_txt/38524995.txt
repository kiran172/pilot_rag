Title: Percutaneous Balloon Dilation of the Pulmonary Valve in a 79-Year-Old With Congenital Pulmonary Valve Stenosis.

Abstract: • Congenital valvar PS is a rare cause of heart failure in the geriatric population. • Transcatheter PV dilation is an effective treatment of congenital PS in older adults. • Cardiac CT can be very useful in the evaluation of suspected valvar PS in adults.
