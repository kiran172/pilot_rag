PMID: 35971787
Title: Does valve morphological type impact TAVR outcomes?

Abstract: Appropriate treatment for the bicuspid aortic valve demands attention to detail across the spectrum of bicuspid morphological types. Transcatheter aortic valve replacement outcomes, while encouraging, require in-depth evaluation before generalization to improve the precision of care.
