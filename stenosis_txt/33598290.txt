PMID: 33598290
Title: Emergency TAVI in a critically ill patient: A case report.

Abstract: Transcatheter aortic valve implantation is a safe procedure even in inoperable patients with multi-organ failure and cardiogenic shock. In such cases, the heart team should be prepared to proceed to emergent implantation for timely and successful management of the patient.
