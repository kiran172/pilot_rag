PMID: 34316981
Title: Successful TPV Implantation in a Pregnant Patient With Right Ventricle to Pulmonary Artery Conduit Obstruction.

Abstract: A patient with repaired double outlet right ventricle presented during early gestation with heart failure symptoms due to severe right ventricle-pulmonary artery conduit stenosis and insufficiency. In the first trimester, she underwent transcatheter therapy with Melody pulmonary valve implantation with excellent hemodynamic results and completed pregnancy without significant maternal complications. (Level of Difficulty: Advanced.).
