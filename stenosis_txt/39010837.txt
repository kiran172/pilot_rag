Title: Type III fetal redundancy of the foramen ovale flap mimics hemodynamic changes of mitral stenosis.

Abstract: Type III redundancy of the foramen ovale flap (RFOF) mimics hemodynamic changes of mitral stenosis(MS), which has not been particularly highlighted in previous literature but carries a favorable prognosis.
