PMID: 37325463
Title: Degenerative Mitral Stenosis: A Case-Based Review.

Abstract: • DMS has a high morbidity and a 5-year mortality rate of >50%. • DMS frequently occurs as mixed mitral disease as well as multivalvular disease. • Assessment of severity requires use of TTE, TEE, and stress echocardiography. • CT is used for periprocedural planning. • Treatment can be surgical or transcatheter.

Citation: Lamberg M, et al. Degenerative Mitral Stenosis: A Case-Based Review. Degenerative Mitral Stenosis: A Case-Based Review. 2023; 7:189-196. doi: 10.1016/j.case.2022.12.014
