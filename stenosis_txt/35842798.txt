Title: Transcaval transcatheter aortic valve implantation in bicuspid aortic valve: A step-by-step procedural guidance.

Abstract: We report the case of a 78-year-old female with Sapien 3 transcatheter heart valve implantation in the transcaval approach. In this setting, we describe the step-by-step management and technique of the transcaval transcatheter aortic valve implantation.
