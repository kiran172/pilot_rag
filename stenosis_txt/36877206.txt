PMID: 36877206
Title: Aortic root replacement with the stentless Freestyle bioprosthesis.

Abstract: In this video tutorial, the technical details for the implantation of the Freestyle stentless bioprosthesis are outlined based on the case of a 76-year-old male patient with symptomatic stenosis of a bicuspid aortic valve and aortic root dilatation.
