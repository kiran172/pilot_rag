PMID: 37983095
Title: Conservative management of pneumopericardium: A case of unexpected success.

Abstract: A 79-year-old male with severe aortic stenosis was admitted with syncope and cranioencephalic traumatism with major nasal hemorrhage. While being prepared for surgical aortic valve replacement, a high-degree atrioventricular block was detected and a definite pacemaker was implanted.

Citation: Gomes ARM, et al. Conservative management of pneumopericardium: A case of unexpected success. Conservative management of pneumopericardium: A case of unexpected success. 2023; 35:E75-E83.
