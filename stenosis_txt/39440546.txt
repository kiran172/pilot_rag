Title: Open Expansion Laryngoplasty for Combined Glottic and Subglottic Stenosis.

Abstract: Expansion laryngoplasty is a new, combined procedure which can treat both glottic and subglottic stenosis simulataneously. This is a small case series showing how to perform this surgery as well as outcomes from a 15-year period. Laryngoscope, 2024.
