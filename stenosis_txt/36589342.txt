Title: Role of Transthoracic Echocardiography in Early Diagnosis of Williams Syndrome in the Neonatal Period.

Abstract: • Williams syndrome with SVAS can develop rapidly. • Diagnosis of WS based on facial features is difficult in neonates. • TTE plays an important role in the assessment of WS in neonates.
