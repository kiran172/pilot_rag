PMID: 34603724
Title: Endovascular management of symptomatic deep vein thrombosis with combined approach.

Abstract: The combined used of thrombectomy with direct intravenous thrombolytic infusion provided effective treatment of DVT and uncovered an underlying left common iliac vein stenosis, which was successfully managed by angioplasty and stenting.

Citation: Gallicchio V, et al. Endovascular management of symptomatic deep vein thrombosis with combined approach. Endovascular management of symptomatic deep vein thrombosis with combined approach. 2021; 9:e04778. doi: 10.1002/ccr3.4778
