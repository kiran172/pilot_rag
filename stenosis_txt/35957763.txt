Title: Infantile hypertrophic pyloric stenosis, the cause of non-bilious vomiting of a 3-day-old male infant with situs inversus totalis: A case report.

Abstract: The synchronicity of situs inversus totalis (SIT) and infantile hypertrophic pyloric stenosis (IHPS) is rare. We have reported a case of this concurrency in a 3-day-old newborn with classic symptoms.
