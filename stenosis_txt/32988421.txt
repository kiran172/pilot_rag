Title: Dealing with kinked and swirled pulmonary vessels: surgical treatment of arterial tortuosity syndrome: a case report.

Abstract: We present the case of a child with arterial tortuosity syndrome, describing the operative findings and our surgical technique to address pulmonary arteries stenosis.
