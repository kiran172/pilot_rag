PMID: 34715699
Title: Black Aorta from Alkaptonuria.

Abstract: A-76-year old male with a past history of alkaptonuria with ochronosis (homogentisic acid deposition in tissues) had symptomatic aortic stenosis. Surgical replacement of the valve was undertaken, and he was noted to have a severely pigmented and porcelain aorta.

Citation: Laurent JP and Galvin SD. Black Aorta from Alkaptonuria. Black Aorta from Alkaptonuria. 2021; 9:165-166. doi: 10.1055/s-0041-1729916
