Title: Symptomatic Recurrent Chylopericardium: How to Manage This Rare Entity?

Abstract: Recurrent chylopericardium after cardiac surgery is a rare entity. This paper presents the case of a 69-year-old female who developed a large recurrent chylopericardium related to surgical myectomy and resection of sub-aortic membrane for hypertrophic sub-valvular aortic stenosis. Treatment required pericardiocentesis followed by lymphangiogram with glue embolization of the lymphatic leak. (Level of Difficulty: Intermediate.).
