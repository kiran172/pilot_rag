Title: Aspergillus tracheobronchitis in COVID-19 patients with acute respiratory distress syndrome: a cohort study.

Abstract: Comprehensive work-up is needed for COVID-19 ARDS patients, especially when suspecting invasive fungal infections. Aspergillus tracheobronchitis has a substantial prevalence in patients with CAPA accounting for an overall mortality of 75% in this study. https://bit.ly/3uF3FZU
