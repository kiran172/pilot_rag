Title: Treatment of Severe Symptomatic Aortic Stenosis During Pregnancy: A Potential Role for TAVR?

Abstract: A 35-year-old woman presented at 22 weeks gestation with severe symptomatic aortic stenosis with a mean gradient of 94 mm Hg and an aortic valve area of 0.53 cm2. After multidisciplinary discussion, she underwent transcatheter aortic valve replacement during pregnancy.
