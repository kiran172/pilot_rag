Title: Papillary muscle rupture after transcatheter aortic valve implantation: A case report and literature review.

Abstract: We present a case of posterior papillary muscle rupture associated with severe eccentric mitral regurgitation following transcatheter aortic valve implantation, that was successfully treated with transcatheter edge to edge mitral repair and review similar cases in literature.
