Title: Is timing everything? Bioprosthetic valve fracture in valve-in-valve TAVR.

Abstract: We present a challenging case of early bioprosthetic valve degeneration following valve-in-valve transcatheter aortic valve replacement and bioprosthetic valve fracture.
