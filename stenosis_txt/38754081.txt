Title: Valve-in-valve implantation to seal post-dilation-induced annular rupture in transcatheter aortic valve replacement.

Abstract: An 85-year-old woman was admitted to our hospital with severe symptomatic aortic stenosis. Preoperative computed tomography and transesophageal echocardiography (TEE) revealed a type I bicuspid aortic valve.
