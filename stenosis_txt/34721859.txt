Title: A rare case of foreign body aspiration presenting as subglottic stenosis: A case report.

Abstract: In rare cases, foreign body aspiration may present with similar clinical features and CT findings to subglottic stenosis. A high index of suspicion and additional diagnostic techniques may be required to distinguish them.
