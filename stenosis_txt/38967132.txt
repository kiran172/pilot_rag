PMID: 38967132
Title: Carotid revascularisation versus medical treatment for asymptomatic carotid artery stenosis.

Abstract: This is a protocol for a Cochrane Review (intervention). The objectives are as follows: To compare the safety and efficacy of carotid revascularisation plus best medical treatment with best medical treatment alone in people with asymptomatic carotid artery stenosis.
