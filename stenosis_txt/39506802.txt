PMID: 39506802
Title: Fungal endocarditis after transcatheter aortic valve implantation complicated with pseudoaneurysm of the ascending aorta.

Abstract: Fungal endocarditis following transcatheter aortic valve implantation (TAVI) is a rare and serious complication of this procedure. We describe a case of a 75-year-old patient who developed fungal endocarditis caused by Candida albicans, complicated by an aortic pseudoaneurysm.
