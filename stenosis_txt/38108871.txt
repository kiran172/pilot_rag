Title: Three-dimensional optical coherence tomography to decide on reverse wire technique in bifurcation interventions.

Abstract: A 79-year-old male with effort angina was admitted to receive optical coherence tomography (OCT)-guided percutaneous coronary intervention for restenosis of an everolimus-eluting stent implanted to the left anterior descending artery (LAD) eight years ago.
