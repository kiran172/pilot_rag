PMID: 32997121
Title: Characteristics and Outcomes of Patients Deferred for Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement Because of COVID-19.

Abstract: This cohort study examines the characteristics and outcomes of patients for whom transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR) was deferred because of the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic.

Citation: Ro R, et al. Characteristics and Outcomes of Patients Deferred for Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement Because of COVID-19.
