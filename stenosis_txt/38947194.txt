Title: Rheumatic Heart Disease: A Rare Cause of Very Severe Valvular Aortic Stenosis.

Abstract: • RHD is a rare cause of severe valvular AS. • Rheumatic MS typically accompanies rheumatic AV disease. • 2D and 3D echocardiography are essential in the assessment of rheumatic AS. • CCT has become crucial in planning therapeutic procedures for AS.
