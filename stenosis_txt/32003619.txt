Title: George Armstrong: pioneer paediatrician.

Abstract: George Armstrong was probably the first physician to practice exclusively as a paediatrician in this country. He published the first account of the postmortem appearance of congenital pyloric stenosis. This year marks the 300th anniversary of his birth.

Citation: Ellis H. George Armstrong: pioneer paediatrician. George Armstrong: pioneer paediatrician. 2020; 81:1-2. doi: 10.12968/hmed.2020.0011
