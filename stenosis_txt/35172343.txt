Title: [Anesthetic and Perioperative Management in Carotis Endarterectomy (CEA) - Part 2: Neurological Assessment].

Abstract: The second part of "Anesthetic and Perioperative Management in Carotid Endarterectomy (CEA)" reviews the apparative/diagnostic and clinical techniques regarding neurological monitoring and perioperative complications. In particular, advantages and disadvantages concerning different methods of neurological assessment will be presented. Perioperative complications and their management will be discussed.
