Title: A new single-use disposable duodenoscope (EXALT™ Model D) for the treatment of an anastomotic biliary stenosis in a liver transplant patient.

Abstract: A 54-year-old male was admitted due to painless jaundice to our hospital four weeks after undergoing liver transplantation. Magnetic resonance imaging cholangiography demonstrated the presence of an anastomotic biliary stenosis.
