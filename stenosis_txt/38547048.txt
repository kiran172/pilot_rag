Title: Transcatheter aortic valve replacement in a patient with quadricuspid aortic valve stenosis and regurgitation.

Abstract: We report the case of an 80-year-old woman with known quadricuspid aortic valve (QAV), severe aortic stenosis, and moderate-to-severe regurgitation who recently underwent a transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR).
