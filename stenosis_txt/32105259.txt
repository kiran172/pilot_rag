PMID: 32105259
Title: [Carotid endarterectomy in acute period of ischemic stroke].

Abstract: A review is devoted to carotid endarterectomy for symptomatic carotid stenosis in acute period of ischemic stroke. Patient selection criteria, dates of surgical intervention and perioperative risk were analyzed.
