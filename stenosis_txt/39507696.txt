PMID: 39507696
Title: Factfinders for patient safety: Epidural steroid injection in patients with lumbar spinal stenosis.

Abstract: This FactFinder presents a brief summary of the evidence suggesting that epidural steroid injections can be safely performed even in the setting of severe, multilevel lumbar spinal stenosis.
