PMID: 34317117
Title: Complete Transposition of Great Arteries With Dominant Left Ventricle: Long-Term Survival in Natural History.

Abstract: We report the case of an adult patient, affected by complete transposition of great arteries with ventricular septal defect, who survived until 68 years of age without surgery, thanks to the presence of a common atrium and pulmonary stenosis. (Level of Difficulty: Advanced.).
