Title: Belt and suspenders: Why it pays to protect and cover during carotid stenting.

Abstract: Carotid stenting results show equivalence to carotid endarterectomy. Most patients undergoing carotid intervention are asymptomatic. Using embolic protection with a covered mesh stent shows promise in this first large multicenter study.
