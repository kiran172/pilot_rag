Title: Hybrid Balloon Mitral Valvuloplasty in an Adult Dog with Congenital Mitral Stenosis and Left Atrial Thrombus.

Abstract: • Congenital MS is a rare cardiac disease in canines. • BMV may improve the mobility of stenotic mitral leaflets. • AF and LA thrombi may occur in congenital MS.
