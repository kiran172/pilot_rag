Title: Creation of a Neo-Mitral Valve With Right Atrial Appendage Tissue in an Infant.

Abstract: Mitral valve replacement in neonates and infants is a challenging operation with few good options. Neo-mitral valve reconstruction with right atrial appendage (RAA) may overcome some of the limitations of existing options.
