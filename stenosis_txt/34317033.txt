PMID: 34317033
Title: Intracoronary Lithoplasty in Percutaneous Treatment of Challenging Calcified Coronary Lesions.

Abstract: Unexpanded stents in calcified coronary stenosis is a problem where intravascular lithotripsy could be effectively employed. In these 2 cases, we report possible issues associated with the use of this technology. (Level of Difficulty: Intermediate.).
