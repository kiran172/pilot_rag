Title: Anomalous right coronary artery originating from the left anterior descending artery with stenosis: a highlighted imaging observation.

Abstract: This imaging observation underscores a rare single coronary artery anomaly, wherein the right coronary artery originates from the left anterior descending artery (LAD). The stenosis in the proximal LAD adds complexity, emphasizing the need for multidisciplinary evaluation and decision-making.
