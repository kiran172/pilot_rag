Title: Coronary Physiology Assessment for the Diagnosis and Treatment of Coronary Artery Disease.

Abstract: Functionally significant coronary lesions identification is necessary for appropriate revascularization. This review aims to provide an overview of the available options for coronary stenosis physiologic evaluation with a focus on the latest developments in the field.
