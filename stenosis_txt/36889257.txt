Title: Complex Coronary Intervention - When Time Is of Essence: Ping Pong Technique.

Abstract: Coronary artery disease (CAD) continues to be a significant cause of morbidity and mortality in the U.S. The prognosis and treatment of which is dependent on various factors including type, size, localization and extent of the coronary plaque and severity of the stenosis. Management of critical ostial left main CAD poses peculiar challenges. The present case report demonstrates a unique percutaneous coronary intervention technique helpful in the management of such complex left main coronary lesions.
