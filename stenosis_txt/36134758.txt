PMID: 36134758
Title: The Role of Sialendoscopy in Parotid Duct Stenosis in Graft-Versus-Host Disease.

Abstract: Sialendoscopy, a minimally invasive procedure for concurrent diagnosis and treatment of salivary gland ductal pathologies, was used to alleviate recurrent left parotid gland swelling and dry mouth in a 58-year-old man with chronic graft-versus-host disease. Laryngoscope, 133:279-281, 2023.
