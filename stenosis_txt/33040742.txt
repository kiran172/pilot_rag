PMID: 33040742
Title: Acute aortic dissection in a patient with Williams syndrome infected by COVID-19.

Abstract: Cardiovascular involvement in COVID-19 has different features. Here we report the ominous fate of a neglected adolescent with Williams syndrome that was infected by SARS-CoV-2 and ended by acute aortic dissection.
