PMID: 32479420
Title: Critical Left Main Coronary Stenosis After Sutureless Aortic Valve Replacement.

Abstract: Our case illustrates the feasibility of high-risk PCI to salvage LMCA stenosis, possibly caused by coronary injury during direct coronary cardioplegia for a recent triple-valve operation.

Citation: Chi WK, et al. Critical Left Main Coronary Stenosis After Sutureless Aortic Valve Replacement. Critical Left Main Coronary Stenosis After Sutureless Aortic Valve Replacement. 2020; 32:E170-E171.
