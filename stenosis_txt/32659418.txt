Title: First Reported Case of Neointimal Hyperplasia in the Midportion of a Polytetrafluoroethylene Dialysis Graft.

Abstract: We report a case of stenosis involving the midportion of a polytetrafluoroethylene graft caused by the infiltration of fibroblasts into the lumen of the graft with the development of fibrovascular tissue.
