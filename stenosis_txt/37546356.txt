PMID: 37546356
Title: Role of Multimodal Cardiac Imaging in Low-Flow, Low-Gradient Aortic Stenosis.

Abstract: • Approximately two-thirds of patients with PLF-LG AS have severe aortic stenosis. • Multimodal evaluation is recommended in the group of patients with PLF-LG AS. • Patients with PLF-LG-AS should be screened for cardiac amyloidosis.
