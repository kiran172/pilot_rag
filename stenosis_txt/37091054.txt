PMID: 37091054
Title: T Waves May Not Be What They Seem!

Abstract: A 39-year-old women who received a transvenous dual-chamber pacemaker in childhood developed 20 years later severe tricuspid valve stenosis induced by the leads and worsened by a pseudo-pacemaker syndrome. (Level of Difficulty: Intermediate.).
