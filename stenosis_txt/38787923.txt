PMID: 38787923
Title: Severe functional mitral regurgitation and cardiogenic shock after transcatheter aortic valve replacement.

Abstract: An 83-year-old woman with symptomatic severe aortic stenosis was referred for transcatheter aortic valve replacement. Diagnostic left heart catheterization documented diffuse 3-vessel coronary artery disease.
