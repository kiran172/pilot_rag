Title: [Modern methods of assessment of physiological significance of coronary lesions: A review].

Abstract: The article describes the main methods of assessment of physiological significance of coronary artery stenoses, their use in clinical practice and future perspectives. New diagnostic methods that are currently under research are discussed.
