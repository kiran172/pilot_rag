PMID: 34917970
Title: Heart Failure in a Child: Multimodality Approach Leading to an Unusual Cause.

Abstract: Heart failure secondary to isolated pulmonary artery vasculitis is rarely described in children. We describe a 10-year-old child who presented with right heart failure symptoms, severe pulmonary hypertension, and bilateral branch pulmonary artery stenosis secondary to isolated pulmonary artery vasculitis. (Level of Difficulty: Advanced.).
