Title: Adrenal and ureteral metastasis of malignant melanoma: A case report and review of the literature.

Abstract: We present a rare malignant melanoma of the right adrenal gland and periureteral adipose tissue leading to ureteral stenosis with the subsequent hydronephrotic transformation of the right kidney and pyonephrosis in a 67-year-old man.
