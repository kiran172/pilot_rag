PMID: 31293050
Title: Transcatheter mitral valve-in-valve implantation in a pediatric patient.

Abstract: An 11-year-old girl, with 25 mm mosaic tissue valve presented with clinical and echocardiographic appearance of stenotic mitral valve prosthesis. Her condition was treated via a transcatheter valve-in-valve implantation using a 23 mm Edwards Sapien 3, with satisfactory outcome.

Citation: Momenah TS, et al. Transcatheter mitral valve-in-valve implantation in a pediatric patient. Transcatheter mitral valve-in-valve implantation in a pediatric patient. 2020; 95:1062-1065. doi: 10.1002/ccd.28390
