Title: [Management of Tracheobronchial Stenoses].

Abstract: Tracheobronchial stenoses consist of a spectrum of conditions that may result in focal or diffuse narrowing of the trachea or downstream bronchial system. The purpose of this paper is to provide an overview of the most commonly encountered conditions in terms of diagnosis and therapeutic options as well as the associated challenges for practitioners.
