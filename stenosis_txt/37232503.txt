Title: Congenital left atrial appendage ostial stenosis in an extremely premature infant diagnosed by transthoracic echocardiography.

Abstract: Congenital left atrial appendage ostial stenosis is a very rare congenital cardiac condition. We present the case of an extremely premature infant with congenital left atrial appendage ostial stenosis diagnosed by transthoracic echocardiographic imaging.
