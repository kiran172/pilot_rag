Title: Novel use of the Perceval sutureless bioprosthesis for pulmonary valve replacement in high-risk endocarditis patients: A case series.

Abstract: We present the first known cases of successful implantation of the Perceval sutureless bioprosthetic valve in the pulmonary position in two high-risk endocarditis patients.
