Title: Echocardiographic Diagnosis of a Subaortic Membrane Attached to the Free Edge of the Right Coronary Cusp of the Aortic Valve.

Abstract: • Subaortic membranes may attach to the free edge of the aortic valve cusps. • Subaortic membranes may occur in isolation without other congenital abnormalities. • TEE may offer better characterization of subaortic membranes.
