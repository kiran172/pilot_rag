PMID: 39483745
Title: Double-Chambered Right Ventricle Presenting in Late Adulthood in a Patient With Left Heart Disease.

Abstract: • DCRV is a rare finding in older adults. • DCRV can be seen in association with a wide range of cardiac abnormalities. • Cardiac imaging characterizes anatomy, hemodynamics, and associated cardiac lesions.
