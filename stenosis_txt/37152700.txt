PMID: 37152700
Title: Rare Cause of Late Left Aortic Sinus Obstruction Following Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement.

Abstract: A 74-year-old woman with a history aortic stenosis with prior transcatheter aortic valve replacement presented with non-ST-segment elevation myocardial infarction secondary to a delayed left coronary sinus obstruction. With physiology and intravascular ultrasound guidance, the patient was treated with stents through the valve struts and to the left main. (Level of Difficulty: Intermediate.).
